Ideas worth spreading.

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events.

About

TEDx DebrecenUniversity is about ideas worth spreading! Today, we are living in an innovating global world, based on new ideas in different areas as technology, entrepreneurship, medicine…etc .

But what does this actually mean? How are our lives really impacted by innovation? Do we define our world – how are we being defined? What’s the consequences of innovation? « DREAM ACT INNOVATE” is looking behind ideas that turns into solutions of some of the biggest untreated problems. To make the life of human beings much easier. Where are we standing now and even more important – where are we heading to?
